Idea behind Renov8.
It starts with a typical problem we have during the renovation.
From finding the right team, managing the budget and making sure the project is done on time was almost impossible.
We have decided to change it by applying the same agile methodology we use in the software development (I know it sounds crazy:)).
1. Each project is evaluated upfront with sprint estimation. (Average renovation 1-2 weeks sprint).
2. Constant cost and time monitoring. Which allows constantly interfere with product shortages, additional costs, changes in the plan.
3. Quality control of the project. The client gets a constant qulity project update.

We have estimated that with Agile Renovation Management the client could save up to 60% of the cost.

Project example: Full renovation of 2 Room apartment:
Scope: New IKEA Kitchen with Washing Machine, Fridge, Stove: 2600 € (Paid in IKEA). Bathroom: New floor tiles, New Bath, New WC. 350 €.
Rooms: Scrape old wallpapers, put new wallpapers and paint all white. Put new floor (laminate). Put a new floor lists in every room. 2000 Euro.
Total cost: Under 5 000 €. In fact only 2,350 €. The rest I paid for IKEA Kitchen.
I have been getting estimate from at least 6 different companies from 8K to 13K in average.:(
So making a renovation with Agile practices gave me at least 60% cost savings. Next steps: I want to make at least 10 more renovations and looking for pilot projects with my friends and peers to build a mobile-first renovation project management tool to turn this into a nice cross-market digital product that many people and renovation companies really need.
